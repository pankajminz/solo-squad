Electrical Engineering Club Aims:

  • 1 To provide a platform for Electrical Engineering students to socialise with their peers and to encourage a feeling of community in the electrical engineering stream..
  • 2 To form a group who can be responsible for liaising with department staff and future students regarding student concerns and subject advice..
  • 3 To improve the image of electrical engineering to a more socially diverse group, with a view to attracting future students and promoting equal gender representation in the stream..
  • 4 To provide an opportunity for new students entering the Master of Electrical Engineering from other Universities to meet their peers and form networks..
